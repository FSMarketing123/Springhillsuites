The Offering

A premier extended-stay asset in
Jacksonville's Deerwood Park corridor

Hodges Ward Elliott ("HWE") is pleased to exclusively offer for sale the fee-simple interest in the 102-suite SpringHill Suites Jacksonville (the "Hotel" or "Property"). Jacksonville has remained a favorite year-round destination spot for travelers and business travelers alike, driven by its leisure amenities, cultural attractions, and continued corporate relocations.

Ideally located in Jacksonville's Deerwood Park / Southside Boulevard corridor just off I-95, the Property is surrounded by leading corporate, healthcare, and retail demand generators. The Hotel offers convenient access to the 1.4-million-square-foot St. Johns Town Center and to Downtown Jacksonville, home to the NFL's Jacksonville Jaguars and EverBank Stadium, as well as the scenic Riverwalk, diverse dining options, and entertainment venues.

Guests additionally benefit from proximity to Jacksonville's 22 miles of white-sand beaches and more than 80,000 acres of parks and outdoor space, further enhancing the city's appeal as a top leisure market. Backed by sustained population growth, a top-ranked job market, and billions in citywide development, the all-suite, extended-stay Hotel is well positioned to capture both corporate and leisure demand within one of Florida's most resilient lodging submarkets.

Investment Highlights

Why Jacksonville. Why Now.

A convergence of location, brand, and market timing defines this offering.

Prime Location in Deerwood Park / Southside Corridor

Situated within one of Jacksonville's most established business corridors, centrally positioned between Downtown and the beaches, with direct access to I-95, JTB (Hwy 202), and I-295. Major employers within 1–5 miles include ADP, Merrill Lynch, Blue Cross Blue Shield, Bank of America, and Johnson & Johnson.

Proximate to Premier Retail & Lifestyle Amenities

A 10-minute drive from the 1.4-million-square-foot St. Johns Town Center — Jacksonville's premier open-air retail destination with 175+ shops and restaurants — and within 20 minutes of 22 miles of white-sand beaches and 80,000+ acres of parks.

Leading Healthcare & Educational Institutions

Surrounded by Mayo Clinic Jacksonville (~9 mi), HCA Florida Memorial Hospital (~4 mi), and Ascension St. Vincent's Southside Hospital (~3 mi), plus Jacksonville University and the University of North Florida, serving 25,000+ students.

Rapid Population Growth Fueling Market Momentum

Metro population surpassing 1.7 million — nearly 10% growth since 2020 — making Jacksonville Florida's largest city by both land area and population.

Top-Ranked Job Market Driving Economic Growth

Ranked the No. 2 hottest job market in the U.S. by The Wall Street Journal; led the nation in corporate relocation from 2022–2023 with 67% net growth. Home to FIS, CSX, and Southeastern Grocers headquarters.

Clean Investment Opportunity

The non-union, fee-simple interest is offered free and clear of existing management and financing, affording new ownership maximum optionality in operation and capitalization.

Strong Brand Affiliation with Marriott Bonvoy

A top-performing all-suite brand within the Marriott Bonvoy system — nearly 271 million members worldwide — with access to Marriott's centralized reservation, marketing, and rewards infrastructure.
